## 👋 Welcome

I am **Dr. Fawad Khan**, a Postdoctoral Researcher at EnergyAI Lab, Korea Institute of Energy Technology (KENTECH), South Korea. 

My research focuses on the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence, Wireless Power Transfer (WPT), and Intelligent Energy Systems**, with applications in smart grids, medical devices, and autonomous systems.

---

## 🔬 Research Overview

My work integrates advanced AI techniques with real-world engineering systems:

* **Wireless Power Transfer (WPT)**
  AI-driven optimization of coil and core design, resonant structures, and efficiency enhancement.

* **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning**
  Deep learning, time-series forecasting, anomaly detection in DC power grids, and optimization using models like XGBoost and Transformers.

* **Computer Vision & Robotics**
  Visual SLAM, UAV navigation, stereo vision, and multi-camera systems.

* **Energy Systems & Digital Twins**
  AI-based digital twin development for monitoring and anomaly detection in power networks.
