## About Me

I received my Ph.D. in Telecommunication Engineering from the University of Engineering and Technology Taxila, Pakistan (2021), where my dissertation focused on automatic person re-identification in multi-camera surveillance systems. After my Ph.D., I joined **Chosun University** as a Postdoctoral Fellow (Dec 2022 – Sep 2023), developing AI-based dental assistance systems. I then moved to **GIST University** (Oct 2023 – Oct 2025), working on AI-optimized wireless power transfer (WPT) systems for implantable medical devices. Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the **Energy AI Laboratory, KENTECH**, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Bumshik Lee, focusing on AI-driven digital twins for anomaly detection in DC power grids.

My research interests include:
- **Computer Vision & Medical Imaging:** Person re-identification, dental imaging, 2D–3D reconstruction
- **Wireless Power Transfer (WPT):** AI-optimized coil design, ANSYS Maxwell simulations, FEA
- **Artificial Intelligence & Deep Learning:** Forecasting, anomaly detection, transformer models
- **Energy Systems:** Digital twins, DC grid monitoring, electricity price forecasting
